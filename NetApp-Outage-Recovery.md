## Advanced Outage Recovery & Protocol Analysis (NetApp)

### Situation
Enterprise customers were experiencing severe storage outages across multi-vendor environments, impacting availability and production workloads.

### Challenge
Rapidly diagnose and restore service across complex, layered infrastructure involving multiple storage protocols and distributed systems under production pressure.

### Actions
- Led deep-dive incident investigations into Sev-1 outages affecting enterprise storage systems.
- Performed packet capture and log analysis across storage and network layers to isolate failure domains.
- Diagnosed interoperability issues across SMB/CIFS, NFS, iSCSI, Fibre Channel, and FCoE environments.
- Identified root causes spanning protocol mismatches, configuration drift, and cross-vendor integration failures.
- Authored and refined SOPs and knowledge base articles to standardize future incident response.

### Results
- Restored critical enterprise services during high-severity outages affecting production customers.
- Reduced recurring incident resolution time through improved diagnostic documentation and patterns.
- Strengthened organizational troubleshooting capability for multi-protocol storage environments.
- Contributed reusable operational knowledge adopted across support workflows.

### Technologies
NetApp, Fibre Channel, iSCSI, NFS, SMB/CIFS, packet analysis, storage networking
