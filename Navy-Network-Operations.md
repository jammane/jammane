## Network Administration & Disaster Recovery (U.S. Navy)

### Situation
Operated within mission-critical Navy network environments supporting both classified and unclassified systems.

### Challenge
Maintain continuous operational readiness across distributed systems while ensuring rapid recovery from outages and hardware or OS-level failures.

### Actions
- Administered enterprise-scale network infrastructure including servers, workstations, routers, and switches.
- Supported over 200 systems across operational networks with strict uptime requirements.
- Executed system recovery procedures during OS corruption and hardware failures.
- Developed and delivered technical training for junior personnel in troubleshooting and systems administration.
- Improved disaster recovery procedures and response workflows for recurring failure scenarios.

### Results
- Restored operational networks within hours following critical system failures.
- Improved team-level troubleshooting capability through structured training programs.
- Increased overall resilience and recovery readiness across supported environments.
- Supported sustained mission readiness across multiple network domains.

### Technologies
Windows Server, networking (routing/switching), disaster recovery, system administration
