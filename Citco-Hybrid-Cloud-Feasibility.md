## NetApp Hybrid Cloud Feasibility & Cost Analysis (Citco)

### Situation
Enterprise storage strategy teams were evaluating whether portions of the storage footprint should be migrated to a NetApp hybrid cloud architecture integrated with AWS. The decision carried long-term implications for infrastructure cost, operational complexity, and migration risk.

### Challenge
As part of a three-person engineering team, evaluate the technical feasibility and financial viability of adopting a hybrid cloud storage model versus continuing expansion of managed datacenter-based NetApp infrastructure.

The study required realistic simulation of migration workflows, infrastructure provisioning, and long-term operational cost modeling under production-equivalent conditions.

### Actions
- Contributed as part of a 3-engineer feasibility team analyzing hybrid cloud adoption for enterprise storage infrastructure.
- Designed and executed AWS-based simulation environments replicating proposed NetApp hybrid cloud architectures.
- Modeled end-to-end migration workflows including provisioning, data transfer, and operational transition procedures.
- Participated in cost analysis covering:
  - AWS storage and compute pricing
  - Data transfer and migration overhead
  - Ongoing hybrid operational complexity and management costs
- Validated real-world migration procedures through simulated execution paths to ensure accuracy of assumptions.
- Delivered findings and participated in follow-up technical explanation sessions with upper management and stakeholders.

### Results
- Determined that projected AWS-based hybrid cloud costs exceeded the cost of continuing to expand and operate NetApp storage infrastructure in managed datacenters.
- Provided validated technical and financial analysis that informed infrastructure strategy decisions.
- Helped reduce risk of unnecessary migration by grounding decision-making in end-to-end simulation data.
- Supported executive understanding through direct technical walkthroughs of methodology and findings.

### Technologies
NetApp ONTAP, AWS (EC2, EBS, storage services), Infrastructure Simulation, Cost Modeling, Hybrid Cloud Architecture Analysis
